FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is NFV Wealth safe to use?

Absolutely. NFV Wealth uses top-grade 256-bit encryption, two-factor authentication, OTP verification on every login, and PIN protection. Your funds and data are protected at all times.

How do I open an account?

Click "Get Started", fill in your basic details, verify your email with an OTP code, and your account is live — all in under 5 minutes with no paperwork required.

What transfer methods are supported?

We support local transfers, international wire transfers, PayPal, Wise, Cryptocurrency (Bitcoin), Cash App, Zelle, Venmo, Revolut, and more.

Are there any fees for transfers?

Local transfers are completely free. International transfers may have minimal fees depending on the method and destination. All fees are shown clearly before you confirm any transaction.

Can I use NFV Wealth on mobile?

Yes! NFV Wealth is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. A dedicated mobile app is also coming soon.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via the live chat widget on your dashboard, or visit our Contact page. We're available 24/7.
